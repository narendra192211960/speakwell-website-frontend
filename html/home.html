Training Tips

Follow these tips for effective speech rehabilitation practice

Quiet Place

Find a calm environment for better AI voice analysis feedback.

Good Mic

Ensure your device microphone is clear and working properly.

Daily Practice

Consistent 10-15 min sessions daily are most effective for progress.

Warm Up

Do simple vocal exercises to relax your jaw before starting.

Listen Carefully

Listen to pronunciation examples multiple times before speaking.

Stay Hydrated

A hydrated throat produces clearer sounds and reduces strain.

Don't Rush

Speak slowly and focus on proper articulation correctly.

Track Progress

Review your accuracy scores to celebrate small wins daily.

Be Patient

Improvement takes time. Stay positive and keep going!

Reminders

Set consistency by using scheduled practice reminders.
